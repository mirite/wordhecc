WordHe.cc

Rules

  • The word of the day can be any eight letter or shorter english word
  • If your guess contains a letter that is in the word, but not at the same position, that letter will be shown as yellow
  • If your guess has a letter at the same position as the word of the day, that letter will be shown as green.
  • If a letter in your guess doesn't occur in the word of the day, that letter will be shown as grey.
  • A letter will also be shown as grey if it doesn't occur as many times in the word of the day as it does in your guess.
  • The "Enter" button will only be enabled if your guess appears in the dictionary.
  • There is (currently) no limit to the number of guesses you can make.
